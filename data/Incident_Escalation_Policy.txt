Incident Escalation Policy

Priority Levels

P1 - Critical
P2 - High
P3 - Medium
P4 - Low

Escalation Rules

P1 incidents:
- Escalate within 30 minutes.
- Notify Incident Manager immediately.

P2 incidents:
- Escalate within 2 hours.

P3 incidents:
- Escalate within 1 business day.

Security incidents must always be escalated to the Security Operations Center.

The L2 Support Team handles complex application and infrastructure incidents.
