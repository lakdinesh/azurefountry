IT Password Reset Policy

Employees can reset their password using the Self-Service Password Reset Portal.

Steps:
1. Navigate to the company password reset portal.
2. Verify your identity using MFA.
3. Create a new password that meets company standards.
4. Passwords must contain at least 12 characters.
5. Passwords must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

If password reset fails three times, contact the IT Service Desk.
