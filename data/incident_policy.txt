Incident Management Policy

An incident is an unplanned interruption or degradation of an IT service.

P1 incidents are critical incidents affecting production services.
P1 incidents must be escalated to L2 Support within 30 minutes.
The Incident Manager must be notified immediately for P1 incidents.

P2 incidents must be escalated within 2 hours.
P3 incidents must be reviewed within 1 business day.
